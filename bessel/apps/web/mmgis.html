MMGIS-shaped fixture host

A fixture in the shape of a surface GIS host (it owns the map and the time cursor), honestly not MMGIS itself; the live integration routes through the PlanDev-MMGIS effort. The embedded Bessel panel mounts only behind the bessel=1 flag.

cursor: (panel off) selection: none

Bessel panel is off; append ?bessel=1 to mount it.
