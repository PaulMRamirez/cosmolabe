# Code of Conduct

Bessel is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. We adopt the spirit and standards of the
Contributor Covenant (https://www.contributor-covenant.org, version 2.1).

In short:

- Be respectful and professional. Critique ideas, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks, in any project space.
- Assume good faith; resolve disagreements technically and transparently.

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take
appropriate, fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior,
up to and including removal from project spaces.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers (see SECURITY.md for the
private contact channel). Reports are handled confidentially.
